World Happiness Report 2022

Introduction

This world happiness report 2022 shows:

1) The happiness level of total of 142 countries

2) How different factors affecting the happiness level of each country

3) The average happiness level in 10 regions of the world

4) Number of countries whose happiness level > world avergae in each region

World happiness level map

This map shows the world happiness level in 2022. This map covers a total of 142 countires in the world, including United States of America, Australia, China and United Kingdom. However, there are some counties' data missing in this map, such as Greenland, Antarctica and Sudan. These missing countries has benn filled with grey color in the map. The legend -- happinessscore categorises the happiness level into 6 categories, ranging from less than 30 to over 70. The countries with higher happiness level will be shown in darker red , like United states of America and Canada, while the countires with lower happiness level will be shown in lighter red , like chad and India.

Factors affecting world happiness level

Social support and GDP

This bubble chart shows the relationship between countries' happiness level and GDP and social support . The x axis represents the GDP, y axis shows the support support. In addition, the countries have been grouped into 10 different regions, represting as different colors on chart. The countries' overall happiness level are represted as the size of cicle . The larger the circle , the higher the happiness level the countries have. It can been seen that the higher the social support and GDP will lead to higher happiness score . There is also a slider showing countries happiness level and a filter to filter different regions

Life expectancy

Freedom

Different regions' average happiness level

This bar chart shows the average happiness level in 10 different regions , including Central and Eastern Europe, Commonwealth of Independent States, East Asia, Latin America and Caribbean, Middle East and North Africa, North America and ANZ, South Asia, Southeast Asia, Sub-Saharan Africa, Western Europe. It can be seen that North America and ANZ has the highest average happiness level among all the regions, while South Asia has the lowest average happiness level. However, some countries' data are missing in South Asia , which might impact the overall happiness level.

Number of countries whose happiness level are above world average level in different regions

The pie chart shows the number of countries whose happiness lebel are above the world average happiness level in 10 different regions. It can be seen that among all the countries whose happiness level greater than world average happiness level, Western Europe occupies almost 30% and Latin America and Caribbean occupies almost 20% , while sub-Saharan occupies the least number of the countries which is only 1.3%.
